# 🚗 Car and Pain

> Less pain. More car. **100% offline**, in your language, accessible to everyone, on your terms.

**Anyone with a car has a lot of pain about it** — fuel that drains your wallet, services you forget until it's too late, expenses that pile up invisibly, and paperwork that expires at the worst moment. **Car and Pain** is an offline‑first mobile app (Android + iOS) that takes all of that pain and turns it into a calm, organized, private record you actually own.

No account. No mandatory cloud. No ads. Everything lives on your device, and you can export or import all of it at any time with a single file.

---

The five things every car owner needs, done properly:

| | Feature | The pain it removes |

## The promises

- **Offline‑first & account‑free** — zero connectivity required, ever. All data on‑device.
- **Your data, your rules** — simple full **export & import**: one backup file (with attachments) plus CSV/JSON per entity. Optional, strictly opt‑in self‑hosted or cloud‑file backup.
- **Truly multilingual, both directions** — LTR **English, German, French** and RTL **Persian (Farsi), Arabic, Sorani Kurdish**, with full right‑to‑left layout, multiple calendars (Gregorian / Jalali / Hijri) and Eastern‑Arabic/Persian numerals.
- **Accessible to everyone** — screen‑reader support (including in RTL), dynamic type, high‑contrast, and colour‑blind‑safe charts.
